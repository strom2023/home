About The STROM 2k23

Unveiling Innovation and Igniting Minds


Get ready to be part of a technological extravaganza like no other - STROM 2k23 is here! Hosted by LICET's Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, this symposium is your gateway to unleashing innovation and connecting with some of the sharpest minds in the field. Brace yourself for a series of competitive events designed to challenge and inspire, all while illuminating your understanding of the latest trends in the world of technology. STROM 2k23 is not just an event; it's a journey towards innovation, knowledge, and unforgettable experiences that will shape your engineering path.
