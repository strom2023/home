BLITZ CIRCUIT

Blitz Circuit

Event Objective:
Sharpen your circuit skills and teamwork in an exciting competition. Solve electronic puzzles, boost problem-solving abilities, and explore the world of circuit fixing with friends. Let's enhance innovation, critical thinking, and hands-on experience together!
Timing : 10.30 to 1

Rules and Regulations:

  • Eligibility:
    • Form teams of 3 or 4 students.
    • Only undergraduate students can participate.
  • Registration:
    • Register early to secure your spot.
    • Provide accurate contact details.
    • Follow registration deadlines.
  • Scoring:
    • Earn more points by solving circuits accurately and quickly.
    • Tougher questions carry higher points.
  • Fair Play:
    • No cheating, copying, or dishonest behavior.
    • Maintain honesty and respect.
  • Professional Conduct:
    • Stay polite and respectful at all times.
    • Avoid any form of bullying.
  • Help Options:
    • Clues available in Rounds 2 and 3.
    • Using clues will reduce your points.
  • Students are asked to bring their ID cards and should follow proper dress code.
  • Only students taking an engineering course are allowed to take part in this event.
